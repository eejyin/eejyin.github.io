**Role:** Topic Investigator &nbsp;&middot;&nbsp; **Timeline:** Dec. 2021 – Nov. 2025

Funded by the National Key Research and Development Program of China (No. 2021YFB2400500).

- Studied the differences and integration between Grid-Forming (GFM) and Grid-Following (GFL) converters.
- Conducted qualitative and quantitative transient-stability analysis via the Equal Area Criterion (EAC) and Lyapunov functions.
- Proposed Virtual Synchronous Generator (VSG)-based converter stabilization control methods.
- Verified results through Hardware-in-the-Loop (HIL) experiments using the RT-Box and PLECS.
