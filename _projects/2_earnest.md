**Role:** Research Assistant &nbsp;&middot;&nbsp; **Timeline:** Aug. 2023 – Present

Part of [An Equitable, Affordable and Resilient Nationwide Energy System Transition (EARNEST)](https://www.energy.gov/sites/default/files/2024-04/CX-029959.pdf), with [CURENT](https://curent.utk.edu/) at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville.

- Developed an *N*–*k* contingency simulation and probabilistic scenario-reduction framework for distribution grids under extreme events.
- Built a comprehensive steady-state and dynamic workflow for resilience assessment.
- Implemented a Monte Carlo–driven K-Medoids scenario-clustering method to improve computational efficiency for resilience planning.
