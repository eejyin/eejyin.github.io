**Role:** Project Investigator &nbsp;&middot;&nbsp; **Timeline:** Jun. 2021 – Dec. 2022

Funded by the Jiangsu Power Grid Corporation Scientific Research Project (No. J2021012).

- Studied K-means-based PV generation-unit clustering and characteristics extraction.
- Constructed a fault self-diagnosis evaluation system for PV to accurately locate faulty panels.
- Proposed a VSG-based inertia evaluation and control method for PV systems to enable friendly grid interaction.
