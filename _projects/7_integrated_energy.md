**Role:** Project Investigator &nbsp;&middot;&nbsp; **Timeline:** Sep. 2019 – Dec. 2020

Funded by the Jiangsu Power Grid Corporation Scientific Research Project (No. J2019082).

- Studied the characteristics of multi-energy flows across different spatio-temporal scales.
- Established a matrix model of the energy hub to describe conversion relationships between multi-energy flows.
- Proposed a power-to-gas (P2G) optimized scheduling model for integrated energy systems based on second-order cone programming (SOCP).
- Verified feasibility, economy, low-carbon performance, and effectiveness using MATLAB/[CPLEX](https://www.ibm.com/analytics/cplex-optimizer) with [YALMIP](https://yalmip.github.io/).
