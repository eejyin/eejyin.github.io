## Summary

This work builds a **matrix model of the energy hub** for advanced adiabatic compressed air
energy storage (AA-CAES) and formulates a **multi-energy-flow optimal scheduling** model on top
of it. Casting the coupling between electricity, heat, and stored air as an energy-hub matrix
keeps the coordinated dispatch compact and lets the storage system be scheduled jointly with the
rest of the multi-energy network.
